INSECT HOUSE

🐜 Discover the fascinating world of creepy crawlies! 🦋

Step into our climate-controlled exhibit featuring hundreds of incredible insect species from around the globe. Learn how these tiny creatures play a HUGE role in our ecosystem.

🔬 Daily talks at 11am and 2pm — get up close with our handlers
